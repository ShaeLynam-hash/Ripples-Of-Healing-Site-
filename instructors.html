Meet the Instructors

Certified, passionate, and deeply committed to your well-being — every instructor is hand-selected for their expertise and their humanity.

Anna Fernandez

Founder & Lead Instructor

Anna discovered the beauty of yoga over 20 years ago while searching for relief from chronic pain caused by scoliosis — and received far more than she ever imagined, on and off the mat. After selling her former business in 2012, she completed Elka Yoga Teacher Training in 2013, igniting a lifelong passion for teaching and a deep commitment to helping others discover their own innate healing power. Yoga not only healed her body — it guided her to embody the art of Living Yoga in every area of life. Anna holds certifications in Hatha, Vinyasa, Children's Yoga, and SUP Yoga, and is a Reiki Level 2 Practitioner. Her classes speak to the body, mind, energy & spirit, creating a safe healing space for Every Body.
