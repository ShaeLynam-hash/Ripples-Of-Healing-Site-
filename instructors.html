Meet the Instructors

Certified, passionate, and deeply committed to your well-being — every instructor is hand-selected for their expertise and their humanity.

Anna Fernandez

Founder & Lead Instructor

Anna has been practicing yoga for over 25 years and teaching since 2013. Her journey began with healing chronic pain from scoliosis and grew into a passion for helping others access their own healing power. She teaches gentle, nourishing classes that create space in the body, mind, and energy.
