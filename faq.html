We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe — we never store your card details on our servers.

We have a rigorous instructor selection process. Every instructor on our platform must hold at minimum a 200-hour Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) certification from a Yoga Alliance-accredited school, plus relevant specialty training for their particular modality. We interview every instructor, observe a sample class, check references, and run background checks. We also gather ongoing feedback from members and have regular quality reviews.
