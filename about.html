About Us

We believe healing is not a destination — it's a ripple that spreads outward from every breath, every movement, every act of self-compassion.

Our Story & Mission

Born From Healing

Ripples of Healing was founded by Anna Fernandez after her own journey healing chronic pain from scoliosis through yoga. What began as a personal practice became a passion — and then a calling to share these tools with the world.

We exist to make transformative yoga and somatic healing accessible to every body, everywhere — guided by real human instructors who bring presence, skill, and heart to every session.

The ripple starts with you. When you heal, you show others it's possible. When you breathe, you invite others to breathe.
